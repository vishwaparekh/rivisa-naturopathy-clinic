Diabetes & Metabolic Health

A steady, routine-based approach to support metabolic health through lifestyle and naturopathy-aligned guidance.

What is diabetes?

Diabetes (commonly Type 2) involves higher blood sugar levels and insulin resistance. It often develops gradually and can improve with consistent lifestyle changes.

Common contributors

  • High refined carbohydrate intake and irregular meal timing
  • Low physical activity
  • Poor sleep and chronic stress
  • Weight gain around the abdomen

How naturopathy may help (supportively)

  • Personalized diet guidance that fits your daily routine
  • Structured habits: sleep, timing, hydration, movement
  • Stress and recovery routines
  • Follow-ups to track progress and refine the plan
